CHEESE OMELETTE (OMELETTE AU FROMAGE)

  • #Ingredients
  • #StepbyStep

Ingredients

4 extra large eggs
fine salt, to taste
fresh ground black pepper, to taste
nutmeg, freshly ground, to taste
1 ounce parmesan cheese, grated
2 tablespoons butter
2 ounces gruyere cheese, grated

Step by Step

1. Break the eggs into a bowl.
2. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
3. Add Parmesan cheese.
4. Beat the eggs with a fork until just mixed.
5. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat.
6. Add the beaten eggs.
7. Use the flat side of the fork to stir the eggs until they start to set.
8. Smooth the top and leave the eggs to cook.
9. Add the Gruyere cheese over the surface.
10. Fold the omelette into thirds and flip it onto a heated serving plate.
